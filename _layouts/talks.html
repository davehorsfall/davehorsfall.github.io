Mental Health Talks

Throughout 2022, I will run free mental health talks on Zoom. The session lasts 1 hour, and explores common stressors for software engineers and data scientists working in research, and gives people an opportunity to answer questions anonymously. The aim is to raise awareness of mental health in the workplace, and reduce the stigma of starting conversations about how we are feeling.

Organisations and individuals can register to join for free.
